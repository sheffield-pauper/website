2026 Season Rules

Points

Over the course of the season, players earn points for their wins on league day each month.

  • Players win 3 points per match win and 1 point per draw.
  • The first tiebreaker for league standings is each player's total 3-0 records, followed by 2-0-1 records, and continuing down until the tie is broken. If there is still a tie for a top 8 spot by the end of the league, the players will organise a one round play-off before the top 8 event.

Schedule and qualifying events

  • All players who attend at least one league day will be automatically registered in the league.
  • League day events are listed on the Schedule page and will occur on the last Sunday of the months January to October, as well as 4 additional scoring days on the second Wednesday of July to October
  • The league has 14 scoring days, only a players top 7 scoring days will count towards their total score. For example, if you play 8 days and score 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, and 8 points, only days with scores of 2-8 would count (the 1 point day would be dropped).
  • The schedule may be adjusted at any time to account for holidays and store availability. Adjustments will be updated on the Schedule page and the Instagram page.

Top 8

The top 8 finishers by the end the league will play a final single elimination top 8 event. This will occur on the second saturday in November, or the Saturday before nationals, whichever is earlier.

Prizes

The main prize for the winner of the top 8 is a bye at this years pauper nationals event, details for this event are generally released mid-year and the event normally takes place in November.

Any additional donated prizing will be distributed as the donator wishes (in 2025, this was a couple of chase rares).
