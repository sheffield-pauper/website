Welcome to the Sheffield Pauper league!

Join at Patriot games for exciting competitive pauper! Earn league points throughout the year to secure your spot in the (very prestigious) league top 8 for prizes and glory.

Whether you're experienced or new to pauper, all are welcome to participate with no (*additional) cost of entry.

*Patriot games themselves charge for entry into their weekly sunday pauper event, but our league days cost no extra :)

Get Involved

Ready to join the action? Here's how you can get involved:

  • Follow us: Stay up to date with league news by following us on instagram. Or joining the wider UK pauper community on discord
  • Join us on league weeks: Participate in our event every three weeks to earn points and move up the leaderboard. Check out our full schedule above!
  • Spread the Word: Invite your friends to join the league and grow the community. The more, the merrier!
