2026 Season Rules

Points

Over the course of the season, players earn points for their wins on league day each month.

  • Players win 3 points per match win and 1 point per draw.
  • The first tiebreaker for league standings is each player's total 3-0 records, followed by 2-0-1 records, and continuing down until the tie is broken. If there is still a tie for a top 8 spot by the end of the league, the players will organise a one round play-off before the top 8 event.

Schedule and qualifying events

  • All players who attend at least one league match are registered in the league.
  • League match events are listed on the Schedule page and will occur on the last Sunday of each month.
  • The league has 10 scoring matches, with a player's worst 5 weeks dropped from scoring.
  • The schedule may be adjusted at any time to account for holidays and store availability. Adjustments will be posted to the Schedule page.

Top 8

The top 8 participents in the league will play a final single elimination top 8. This will occur on the second saturday in November, or the Saturday before nationals, whichever is earlier.

Prizes

Traditionally the main prize for the UK leagues has been a bye at nationals.

When nationals is confirmed, this will become the main prize for the winner.

Additional prizing is not guaranteed, but anyone is free to donate.
